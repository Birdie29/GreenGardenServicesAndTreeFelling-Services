Blue Lily

Too much fertilizer and water harms a Lilys ability to brethe. Before you smother it with too much love let us help you fix that. Lilys are considered easy to grow heres a quick tip: Watering regularly during the growing season ensures success and it's crucial to avoid fertilizing your Lilys with high protein fertilizers-we prefer a balanced fertilizer such as a 10-10-10 or 5-5-5 or slightly higher in phosphorus than nitrogen. We also recommend suspending any fertilizer to either potted or in-ground plants by late summer. Too much information? Don't worry our best gardeners will be on the job!!

Bird of Paradise

Is your bird of paradise not blooming and has brown hedges? You may not be giving it proper sunlight and overwatering it. While they do look pretty to look at it's essential to know where to properly grow a bird of paradise as it thrives in full sun to partial shade whereas in the afternoon it may benefit from some shade to protect it from scorching, helping it to bloom further. Also the plant prefers consistent moist soil but can tolerate short periods of drought. Overwatering causes root rot so it's essential that we do weekly check ins to water springly and allow the soil to dry out slightly between the waterings. Give us a call we can hear your green babies silent cry for some sunlight!
